About Us

Parkin Lawn Care has been servicing Peterborough, Millbrook, Clarington and surrounding areas since 2021.

We offer a weekly lawn cutting service and, once you sign up we will set a day for service and you can expect us the same day each week. Our staff are friendly, reputable, compassionate, and eager entrepreneurs. We take the time to get to know you, and respond to requests in the easiest and most efficient way possible. Our business is built on trust with our customers. If we don't build and maintain trust with our customers than we believe it wouldn't be fair to our customers. We look forward to hearing from you soon.
